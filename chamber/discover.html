About Us

The oldest chamber in the state of Montana, we have been in operation since 1890. We are made up of local citizens dedicated to fostering good business in the Helena area. Our Chamber also operates the Convention & Visitor's Bureau (CVB) for the city of Helena. We are committed to increasing tourism in Helena, and ensuring local businesses benefit from tourism to our community.
